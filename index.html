Projects

During my undergraduate degree I have been involved on a variety of academic and work-related products. See some samples of my work below

Ticket to Mars Android App

Ticket to Mars was team project to build an Android Application based on the board game Ticket to Ride. We used various design patterns, such as the Observer Pattern, the Proxy Pattern, and the Plugin Pattern. We also learned how to create UML Diagrams.

MECPro Software

On campus I work as a computational research assistant. I helped build a piece of software called MECPro which is used to learn more about spin state crossover during organometallic reactions.

PFEEF Website

During a Study Abroad/Internship, I helped build a Wordpress Website for a company in the UK called Personal Finance Employee Education Fund (PFEEF). PFEEF is a business to business organization that coordinates with employers to teach financial literacy in the workplace.
